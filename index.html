OUR STORY
Born at Sea.
Built on Experience.

Vault was born from real frustration. Managing a superyacht is extraordinarily complex — and the tools available were built for offices, not vessels crossing oceans.

Crew information lived in email threads. Maintenance logs were on paper. Budget tracking meant twelve different spreadsheets scattered across a dozen folders no one could find under pressure.

We set out to change that. Not by digitising the chaos, but by rethinking how a vessel should be managed from the ground up.

"Built by Crew, for Crew."
— THE VAULT PRINCIPLE
THE PROBLEM
Managing a Superyacht is a full-time operation — with part-time tools.

The superyacht industry runs on institutional memory, mobile communication threads, and outdated spreadsheets. When crew turn over, knowledge walks out the door.

No single source of truth
Crew docs in email, maintenance on paper, budgets in Excel, trips in WhatsApp — no connection between any of them.
Compliance failures under pressure
Expired certificates, overdue inspections, missing documentation — discovered at the worst possible moment.
Crew turnover destroys institutional knowledge
Each new Chief Stew or Engineer starts from scratch. Months of operational context, gone overnight.
THE SOLUTION
One Platform.
Every Department.
Total Clarity.

Vault consolidates every operational function of a superyacht into a single connected system — from the bridge to the galley, from crew compliance to guest preferences.

Real-time compliance tracking
Certificates, inspections, and crew docs monitored automatically with automated alerts.
Unified financial control
Budget vs actual, invoices, and expenses — across every department, every voyage.
Institutional knowledge — preserved
Crew change doesn't mean context loss. Every preference, procedure, and history stays in Vault.
